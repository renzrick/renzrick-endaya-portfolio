“Elevate” Skin Care Product Poster

A 3D product mockup poster using Adobe Dimension

My Role

Lead Designer

Challenges

The main challenge was to create a 3D product mockup poster using Adobe Dimension

Once the initial design is compeleted, I then have to import the Dimension file into Adobe Photoshop to further enhance the rendered composition.

Brainstorming

After careful consideration as to what should be the main pinnacle of my design, I decided to create the brand “Elevate” — a self-curated skin care line.

Rather than just highlighting one product, I also decided to implement multiple different skin care products within my design to further enhance variety within a poster that had a main intent of being quite minimal. Once the brainstorming process was complete, I underwent the process of creating the rough drafts for the project.

Rough Drafts

I decided to create my own designs for the brand through Adobe Illustrator. Creations of the brand guide and logo design were processed first, as I did product research and developed brand colours and fonts. I also hand-drew multiple rough iterations of the brand’s logo.

Once the brand guide and logo design processes were complete, I designed a series of product labels that would be shown on the 3d modeled containers for each specified skin care product. There were a total of 4 product labels created.

Asset Creation

With the branding aspect complete, I was now able to undergo the process of creating the assets that would be on the final poster. Each 3D modeled container were curated through the help of the starter assets that were already provided within the Adobe Dimension software. However, I chose to modify the containers to my preferred speculations. Once the containers were completed, the product labels were then applied onto each specified container and its box. Implementations of artificial lighting were also presented within the design, to further show a sense of realism within the composition as a whole.

A background was then applied within the software then brought into Adobe Photoshop to further strengthen the composite overall. I applied the brand’s logo as well as a tagline as the poster’s main focal point. A few more refinements were applied in terms of colour-correction, placement of the products, 3d rendering, etc.

Conclusion

Overall, I am proud of this project — mainly because of the fact that I felt that I believe there are many factors within this project that present a generally good design. I also believe that this project can highlight my attention to subtle detail, while also following essential design principles.
