About Me

Renzrick Endaya

Multi-disciplinary artist

“I want to be able to create connections worldwide through my industry work as well as my personal designs within the New Media field.”

Aside from his passion for graphic design, Renzrick also takes interest in both the fashion and music industries. The creativity shown within the two also sparks his imagination as a whole. To add onto his infatuation with music, Renzrick is also a self-taught music producer, however, he has not released many compositions onto streaming platforms as of right now – but he is currently in the midst of creating a collection of songs that can be hopefully released in the near future!

Renzrick is currently studying in BCIT’s New Media and Web Development program, located in Vancouver, BC.
